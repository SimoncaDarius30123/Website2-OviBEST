PRODUCTS / SLIDING SYSTEMS PVC

Doors with lifting and sliding system

The doors with the lifting and sliding system offer you a spacious and bright atmosphere. Whether for a balcony, veranda or terrace, with one turn of the hand you can create a seamless connection between the inside and the outside - there are no annoying threshold elements. Like all Salamander profile systems, the lift and slide door also has a high degree of shape stability.

The balanced structural design of the door ensures an easy movement of the leaf and guarantees a durable and reliable functionality.

Features :

-high quality, and in a varied range of colors

- weatherproof and particularly easy to handle thanks to the branded vinyl with a very dense surface structure.

- the intelligent design of the system allows rational production.

- multi-chamber profiles and a thermally insulated threshold provide optimal insulation.

- large steel supports ensure maximum stability, reliable operation and long life.

Doors with JETLINE closure

Thin lines create a shiny look.

Jetline makes rooms look more spacious and bright. The profiles in the threshold area have a straight design and are therefore particularly easy to clean. The balanced structural design of the door ensures easy movement of the leaf and guarantees durable and reliable operation. Visually, Jetline also meets the highest requirements and can be harmoniously combined with the profile lines of the SIP window ranges.

Features :

- maximum stability, large steel reinforcements ensure safe functionality and a long service life.

- easy to clean thanks to the straight profile design in the threshold area.

- the high-quality foil coating comes in a wide range of colors.

- weather resistant and particularly easy to treat thanks to the branded vinyl with a very dense surface structure.
