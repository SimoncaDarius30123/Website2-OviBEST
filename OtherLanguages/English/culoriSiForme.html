PRODUCTS / COLORS AND SHAPES

Salamander profiles meet the requirements of the most creative and demanding architects, emphasizing the modern style, but also offering the possibility of renovating historic buildings by complying with protection norms.

On the aluminum side, the sky's the limit when it comes to colors. RAL codes, hundreds of shades, color combinations, wood imitations, anodized colors, etc.

Nothing is impossible: together with specialists, the solutions come naturally, ennobling the facades and interiors, leaving free the possibility of creation and putting your ideas into practice.

Classic shapes, arches, segmented, asymmetrical windows, triangles, round windows, large windows, sliding doors, the possibilities are endless. Everything is possible. You can find the colors of the forest, the shades of the sand with the help of profiles in natural shades, you can bring color into your home with windows in cheerful tones.

Or you can simply customize the outside of the windows in accordance with the architecture, leaving the white on the inside to allow different layouts in different rooms.

We are an engaged and dedicated team focused on excellence and innovation. We focus on customer satisfaction by providing the best products and services, always looking for ways to adapt and develop to meet the ever-changing demands of the market.
