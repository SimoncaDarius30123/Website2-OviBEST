PRODUCTS / ROLLS

Overlapping rolls

The roll box is made of PVC and is provided with polystyrene for thermal and sound insulation. This type of roller has three constructive heights of 165mm, 205mm, 240mm.

The rolls can be white, brown or wood imitation.

Thermal insulation with polystyrene improves the coefficient of thermal and sound insulation of the box and prevents the formation of condensation.

It provides solar protection that stops up to 80-85% of the energy carried by the sun's rays.

It is composed of a box, PVC guides and a carpet of aluminum slats, the slats being insulated with foam.

Access to the inner part of the box is achieved through its lower part.

The roller is fixed to the window frame with the help of a PVC profile, and on the side with metal clips, giving it good rigidity.

Rolls overlapped with mesh/Thermoprestige

The rolls overlapped with mesh are composed of a box, PVC guides and a carpet of aluminum slats. The slats are insulated with foam, and the PVC box has polystyrene thermal insulation.

The heat transfer coefficient is 0.80 k/m2.

Access to the inside of the box is through its lower part.

The roller is fixed on the window frame with the help of a PVC profile, and on the side with metal clamps, which gives it good rigidity.

The anti-insect protection system is incorporated in the same box as the slatted carpet and slides between the window frame and the slatted carpet.

Applied rolls

They bring you extra thermal and sound protection, significantly reducing energy costs both in winter and in summer. Once the roller blind is lowered, the privacy and safety of your family will no longer be a problem. Moreover, the outer roll offers extra protection against dust and the weather that may occur.

The applied rolls consist of a box, guides and carpet of aluminum slats. These aluminum slats are foam insulated. They are easy to install, use and maintain.

This kind of roller blinds can be mounted on existing windows.

For overlapping rolls, we offer the following types of box finishes:

box visible from inside and outside

box visible only from the outside, the inside can be plastered

box visible only from the inside, the outside can be plastered

the box can be plastered both inside and outside
