PRODUCTS / GARAGE GATES

Sectional garage doors

Whether you are building a house or just renovating, you will probably need a garage door, and our recommendation would be to opt for a sectional garage door. You will save space because sectional garage doors open vertically, sliding parallel to the ceiling, which allows you to gain space outside and inside the garage. They are safe and durable, being built in such a way as to protect the car and the goods for many years.

Industrial gates

We sell a wide range of industrial doors. They are manufactured and checked according to the most rigorous standards in the field, to ensure operational safety, thermal and sound insulation. They are equipped as standard with safety devices against breaking springs and against breaking cables according to European standards.
